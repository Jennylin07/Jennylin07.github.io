    How Rabbits make great pets!

    You can bring it anywhere you want! You can even bring it to the beach when you tan, the rabbit will sit right next to you quietly. It won't run away! They are not the easiest to clean up after, but they will keep you company. Isn't it adorable?

    Supplies

    It's so simple! You simply need just a roomy cage just a little big bit bigger, so the rabbit can move around. It need's a litter box and pellet bowl just in case it needs to pee or poop. Give it a water bottle, so it does not thrist to death! Provide some toys for the rabbit to play with, so it does not die from boredom.

    Remember to brush your rabbit!

    First, calm down your rabbit, so it's not scared. Place it on a towel or your lap. Gently brush with a soft comb the direction of the fur and remember to pluck out the loose fur. Then, brush off any excess fur!
